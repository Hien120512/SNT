Risk Analyst

New York City Metro (Hackensack, NJ, United States) Position: 11112
The Company:
Risk Management Solutions (RMS) is the world's leading provider of solutions for the management of catastrophe risks.  The models and consulting services provided by RMS are used by insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations, and governments to assess a wide-range of natural and man-made perils such as hurricane, earthquake, flood, windstorm, terrorism, and disease pandemic.  Founded at Stanford University in 1988, RMS continues to grow and diversify in order to service and meet the needs of its clients. 
 
The Opportunity
 
RMS is looking for a team of Risk Analysts to join their Analytical Services team.  As a Risk Analyst, you will enjoy substantive responsibility that comes with an entry level position in the Client Development organization within RMS, as well as benefit from a training program encompassing the key business elements of RMS' risk-based activities.
After receiving a month of structured training, you will begin an immersive program of learning and applying RMS products and services.  Through this program:
  • You will provide clients with wide-ranging support and consultancy regarding the use and interpretation of RMS' analytic tools. 
  • You will learn how RMS manages and grows specific client accounts at both the working and strategic/commercial level. 
  • You will conduct analytical and consulting work to assess the risk of financial loss due to natural and man-made catastrophes for private client placements as well as the potential to be involved with publicly traded securitizations.
  • You will visit clients and experience first-hand meetings with their catastrophe management staff. 
  • You will be heavily involved in the manipulation and quantification of client data using a variety of database tools, analyzing and interpreting data using RMS' technologies as well as working with other RMS departments and clients. 
 
Through this experience, you will gain an understanding of the analytical role of natural disaster risk management in the underwriting, portfolio management, and capital allocation processes of the insurance sector.  This is an excellent opportunity for the right candidate to join a challenging team-oriented and consultative environment that fosters career growth and development.
 
Required Qualifications:
 
The successful candidate should have the following attributes and skills:
  • Strong analytical and business problem solving ability
  • Mathematical and statistical understanding
  • Excellent written and verbal communication
  • Willingness to travel
  • Strong database and data manipulation skills - working knowledge of Excel, VBA, SQL, or Access is an advantage
  • Applicants should have B.A. or B.S. with honors, or M.S/M.B.A/MEng in an analytical or numerate discipline, ideally with a background in mathematics, engineering, statistics, economics, GIS, or natural sciences
  • Knowledge of insurance, reinsurance, or financial institutions is an advantage
The successful candidate will have an inquisitive mind, be organized, able to meet deadlines and thrive in a team environment. They must also be prepared to be individually accountable and make a valuable contribution to client or project outcomes.
