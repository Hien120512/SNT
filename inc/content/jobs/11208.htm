Technical Writer

Objective of the Technical Writer 
 
This position will be required to work in a fast paced environment with a multi-disciplinary team of analytical professionals working on the commercial and residential building attributes data product. Candidate will work closely with international team in India and US and must be able to write, edit, index, or revise a variety of technical, user documentation such as processes manuals and reports for a wide range of uses. In addition,  theposition is required to analyze requirements of project to determine types of publications needed

 
Key Accountabilities & Deliverables:
 
  • Organize material and complete writing assignment according to set standards regarding order, clarity, conciseness, style, and terminology.
  • Review existing materials and recommend revisions or changes in scope, format, content, and methods of reproduction and binding
  • As an integral part of cross-functional product teams, must gain knowledge of the processes and product thoroughly
  • Maintain records and files of work and revisions
  • Study product specifications product to integrate and delineate technology, operating procedure, and production sequence and detail.
  • Work with Subject Matter Experts (SME) to gather information to include in the documentation.
  • Coordinate the distribution and review of drafts and organize review meetings as and when required.
  • Interaction with analytical and database professionals working on the commercial and residential building attributes data product.

  • Advanced computer skills mainly MS Word, AuthorIT, Photoshop and Paintshop PRO. HTML experience is also desired
  • Knowledge or experience in Property Insurance or Reinsurance sector and Risk Assessment Analysis is desirable
  • Exposure to US or Indian commercial or residential building construction practices
  • Good statistical analysis skills and good GIS knowledge
  • Must be flexible and have ability to interact with all levels in the organization
  • Highly motivated individual able to grasp complex processes and learn quickly
  • Conceptual understanding of relational databases, web technologies and software developed at enterprise level
  • Ability to drive the necessary processes and standards to manage and produce the quality documentation.
