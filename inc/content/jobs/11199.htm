Senior Manager Data Analytics

New Delhi, India (Noida, India) Position: 11199
Department

Objective of the Manager/Senior Manager Data Analytics
This position will be required to work in a fast paced environment with a multi-disciplinary team of analytical professionals working on the commercial and residential building attributes data product. Candidate will work closely with international team in India and US and be able to communicate effectively within and outside organization. He is required to perform detailed statistical analysis on large datasets and the insight from this analysis will be used to drive significant decisions.

 
Key Accountabilities & Deliverables:
Specific responsibilities include:
  • Statistical and predictive analysis
  • Provide professional skills necessary for all phases of data analysis, including the application of standard, descriptive and inferential statistical methods for conducting analysis
  • Execute predictive analysis by extracting useful information from structured and unstructured data and use it to envisage future trends and behavior patterns
  • Analyze large datasets using advanced statistical methods, interpret results in order to identify significant differences, relationships and trends in data and publicize written summaries of data analysis to share with senior leadership as appropriate.
  • Serve as an active participant on cross-functional project teams and provide guidance and training to teams in asking appropriate questions, interpreting data, and translating into actionable insights
  • Provide leadership in the identification and description of data elements and make recommendations regarding the creation of analytical reports to achieve client and business objectives
 
 
 
Experience Required:
  • Post graduate engineer in any engineering or analytical discipline, with hands on experience in statistical analysis on large and complex datasets
  • 7 to 10 years of total experience with minimum 3-5 years experience on engineering or analytical projects requiring comprehensive data analysis and interpretational skills
  • Strong SQL knowledge and experience, ability to write custom queries of medium to high complexity.
  • Advanced statistical skills and experience with advanced statistical software package like SPSS, SAS, Minitab, etc.
  • Experience of working on MS Windows, MS Word, Excel, MS Project, MS Visio and PowerPoint
  • Exceptional business insight and strategic orientation
  • Critical thinking skills in formulating hypotheses, interpreting results, and being able to make educated guesses when data may be sparse or unavailable
  • Intellectual curiosity and ability to handle high levels of ambiguity
  • Ability to work in a multi-cultural and matrix organization with multiple stakeholders across different time zones
  • Must have through knowledge of statistical techniques and methodology, variable coding  and data manipulation
  • Detail-oriented and persistent, able to drive complicated tasks through to completion.
  • Ability to perform in a multi-tasking, team-oriented environment and contribute independently
  • Capable of handling high performance teams and proactive at managing relationships
  • Energetic and enthusiastic and have a passion for excellence
