Director of Networking & Security

San Francisco Area (Newark, CA, United States) Position: 11068
Overall Charter:
Reporting to the Vice President of Information Technology in Newark, CA, you will manage the overall Networking & Security function for a high technology company with multiple office locations throughout the globe.
 
Key Objectives & Responsibilities:


  1. Overall responsibility for managing on a 7x24 basis all day to day activities for networking, telephones, video conferencing and security.
  2. Proactively manage capacity planning for network utilization using operational and strategic metrics.
  3. Partner with the Software Development organization on new product dependencies for networking and security.
  4. Partner directly with the Client Development organization and provide guidance on customer contracts as they relate to security.
  5. Vendor management of all contracts associated with WAN’s, LAN’s and security.
  6. Conduct security audits within RMS leveraging third party vendors. This will include penetration testing.
  7. Project / Program Management in an enterprise function.
  8. Manage, develop, mentor and coach a small but senior team of networking and security personnel.
  9. Work with Finance and Accounting to develop appropriate ROI on programs and projects related to networking and security.
10. Enhance and maintain current policies on networking and security.
11. Enhance and maintain RMS BCP plan as it relates to networking and security.
 
 
Requirements: Experiences
  • Bachelor’s Degree, preferably a B.S.; Masters or MBA preferred.
  • Knowledge and direct experience in managing corporate networks and security for 5 years minimally.
  • A minimum of 8 years of progressively more responsibility in the area of IT Management for mid-size corporation.
  • Experience in both small (less than 1,000 employees) and large (greater than 2,500 employee) environments
  • Management of local and extended teams.
 
 
 
 
 
Requirements: Competencies and Capabilities of the Successful Candidate
 
  • Understands the Business:
Can quickly develop an understanding of the elements of the company and the value chain of the business and of customers as well as the external and industry factors that influence success and how Information Technology can play a role in providing support to make these elements successful; can influence key leaders in business unit’s.
 
  • Strategic Thinking:
Develop an overall Networking and Security strategy which aligns to the company’s ambition. Has the ability to develop and articulate a vision of the future in a way that creates energy, excitement and participation; can partner with other leaders in building winning business strategies;  
 
  • Talent Manager:
Is an effective assessor and developer of individuals; Build up a strong core team to help manage the Networking and Security function globally.
 
  • Personally Credible: 
Models the highest degree of moral and ethical behavior; is proactive – takes responsibility for making things happen; contributes as a valued team member and colleague; demonstrates personal awareness and desire for continual learning and personal development; keeps commitments; exhibits candor and courage – is not afraid to establish a visible presence and point of view, to engage in spirited and constructive debate and to hold others accountable to their commitments.
