Objective of the Lead Database Architect
This position will be required to work in a fast paced environment with a multi-disciplinary team of analytical professionals working on the commercial and residential building attributes data product. Candidate will work closely with international team in India and US and be able to communicate effectively within and outside organization. The objective of role is to lead the Data Architecture requirements, contribute to the advancement of data architecture roadmaps, data modeling and data warehousing capabilities and technologies for ensuring the continuous improvement of tools, techniques and the delivery of service to the business.
 
Key Accountabilities & Deliverables:
  • Database design and development
  • Design, develop, and implement infrastructure to provide highly-complex, reliable, and scalable database to meet the organization's objectives and requirements
  • Analyze business requirements for database design, and implement changes to database as necessary
  • Design and architect the centralized databases to enhance the efficiency and optimize the workflow
  • Track the changes of different versions and processes of product database
  • Re-architect and optimize database for better performance and storage space
  • Develop strategies for data acquisitions, archive recovery, and implementation of a database
  • Perform systems analysis on database, and resolve performance, capacity, and replication issues as necessary
  • Develop methodologies, report views, queries, and table replications, and ensures that all the data is in the proper format
  • Provide detailed design and specification documentation, including flowcharts, for all aspects of the database
  • Data Warehouse
  • Assist in data modeling for DWH databases (star-schemas for reporting, OLAP cubes)
  • ETL design to move data between the ODS and DW
  • Participation in the identification, prioritization, and development of technical initiatives and strategies.
  • Provide training to the team members in DWH, BI, ETL and other database concepts
Management:
  • Work in conjunction with and provide leadership and direction to a team of database developers to meet technical requirements
