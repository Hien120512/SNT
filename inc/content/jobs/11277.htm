Marketing Manager

London (London, United Kingdom) Position: 11277
RMS is the world's leading provider of analytics and decision science solutions for the quantification and management of catastrophe risks.  The models and consulting services of RMS are used by hundreds of insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations, and governments to assess a wide-range of natural and man-made perils such as - earthquake, flood, windstorm, terrorism, and disease pandemic.  RMS continues to grow and diversify in order to service and meet the requirement of its clients through research and technology innovation and superior client service.  The company's strength lies in its ability to use and develop the skills of its people across a wide remit of business activities.
 
Department: 
Corporate Marketing team is part of the global Client Development organisation, reporting to the Director of Corporate Marketing in London
 
Objective of the Marketing Manager:
Responsible for driving the strategy and implementation of RMS’ flagship conferences, seminars, and client events in Europe (including online events), as well as supporting internal events in the U.S. and other regions.  Strategic decision making and management of RMS’ presence at external industry events globally to ensure maximum return on investment and strong alignment with corporate and sales objectives.  Supporting RMS sales and marketing campaigns through the generation of value-based sales materials to increase sales and renewals as the company continues to expand its offerings and global presence.
 
Key Accountabilities & Deliverables: 
  • Liaising with senior management to understand objectives and advise on events strategy
  • Planning and overseeing the organisation of internal events, including budget management, venue sourcing, vendor management, entertainment, communication, and management of registrations 
  • Developing conference agendas with senior management and speaker sourcing
  • Organising practice presentations and providing feedback to speakers; reviewing presentation material for style and messaging consistent with the RMS brand
  • Identification and evaluation of global market events to advise on RMS attendance, event sponsorships or exhibitions. Planning and overseeing RMS attendance at external events 
  • Negotiating with external conference organisers on speaking opportunities and sponsorship packages. 
  • Liaising with product marketing and sales to advise on value-based messaging and supporting sales with the generation of materials including talking points, presentations etc.
  • Organisation and dissemination of marketing and sales materials via a centralized content repository
  • Where needed, execution of additional marketing activities to support client satisfaction, PR, branding, and marketing campaigns
  • Some travelling required.
 
Experience Required:
  • Good Degree from a leading University
  • Substantial experience of managing internal and external events in a corporate marketing department or marketing agency within the financial services or B2B sector
  • Implementation of integrated marketing programs to drive leads, increase brand awareness and enhance client retention
  • Management of multiple, complex timetables successfully in a collaborative extremely fast paced, changing environment
  • Proven track record of successful event ROI and client satisfaction
  • Direct or indirect management of support staff an advantage
  • Strong knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and FrontPage required. Familiarity with Adobe InDesign a plus.
  • Working knowledge of event management programs, such as Cvent.
