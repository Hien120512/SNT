Senior Director, Data Solutions

San Francisco Area (Newark, CA, United States) Position: 11039
The Opportunity
The Senior Director, Data Solutions, is responsible for developing and executing a strategy that enables higher quality and more trustworthy decisions in underwriting, portfolio management, and risk transfer, increasing RMS’s value proposition in the P&C insurance space. This will be accomplished through leveraging expertise to build a scalable data and data management business within the context of an analytical business; creating strategies for new markets and increasing share in existing markets; developing an intimate understanding of client business needs and creating in-house as well as partnership data capabilities to address those needs; and anticipating market opportunities and synergies in order to lead the market to the RMS solutions.
 
 
Specific responsibilities
 
Strategy
  • Collaborate strongly with the RMS executive team to evolve the Data Solutions vision, provide input into key strategic decisions, and chart the course forward to achieve RMS’s ambitions
  • Identify non-linear opportunities to improve data and information, drive growth that capitalizes on the dynamics of the insurance market, and monetize existing data assets
  • Prioritize growth and investment options against relevant economic and strategic criteria that balance near and longer-term success
  • Orient the strategy and course correct for an evolving market
Business development
  • Lead business development activities with partners and providers, determining value and fit through explorative engagements and considering end-user value, margins, and channel value
  • Create business cases and perform robust build, buy, and acquire analyses
  • Closely monitor the market pulse and identify new opportunities by building and maintaining senior connections in the industry
Execution
  • Drive the realization of sales targets by developing robust go-to-market plans and collateral
  • Prove the value of business cases through proof of concept client engagements
  • Collaborate with Client Development, Software Product Management, and other RMS teams
  • Meld industry knowledge with prior experience to avoid pitfalls and strategic box canyons
 
 
Qualifications
 
Education
  • Advanced degree in a quantitative discipline required
  • MBA preferred
Experience
  • A demonstrated leader in the data industry with 9+ years of experience
  • A track record of success building a scalable data and data management business within the context of an analytical business
  •  Have “owned” a product line with P&L with 5+ years of product management leadership experience and progressively challenging responsibilities
  • In-depth experience with regards to data technology, including: market drivers of adoption; competitive threats; valuation and structuring of partnerships; relevant industry success and failure lessons; and the impact of rapidly changing technology
  • Experience and proven success selling products or services to large organizations within the insurance, business intelligence, or IT outsourcing markets
Personal traits
  • A strategic thinker, with the ability to grow the business over a multi-year time frame
  • Creative in conceptualizing and building-out innovative data strategies
  • A strong leader who can build consensus across multiple constituencies and lead cross-functional teams
  • Collaborative and client-focused, yet willing to take a stand and push back when appropriate
  • Exceptional oral and written communication skills, to enable effective communication with all levels of the organization and with RMS clients
  • Inquisitive and intellectually curious
  • Willingness to travel domestically and internationally
