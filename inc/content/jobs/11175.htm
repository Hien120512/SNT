Senior ETL Engineer
You will be part of team responsible for designing and developing the critical business applications in the next generation software suite.  You will use best-of-breed technology from both established vendors such as Microsoft as well as open source technology.  As a Senior ETL Engineer, this will be an opportunity to play a key role in designing, developing, and furthermore innovating together as you bring your considerable software engineering skills to our dynamically growing organization.

Additionally, as a Senior ETL Engineer, you will focus on the following areas:
  • Provide a senior-level contribution to a team responsible for the design, development and implementation of critical enterprise scale applications on a multi-terabyte environment.
  • Collaborate with engineers, Product/Project Managers and Quality Assurance engineers to develop multi-tier, multi-layered application layer framework and functional components, following coding, documentation and design standards
  • Innovate, design and develop extensible and reusable applications to specification, for the enterprise suite
  • Participate in internal/cross team meetings, requirements gathering, scoping, decision making and technical documentation
The Software Development group focuses on developing and building cutting edge, high performance enterprise software solutions that deliver risk quantification tools to our 400+ clients worldwide. Using cutting edge technology, we are building an exciting next generation product that leverages the latest advances in database tools, grid computing, hardware-based acceleration techniques, and visualization optimization. Our Enterprise Platform utilizes restful web services, 100+ TB of data warehousing/OLAP solutions, large scale distributed data processing technology, best-in-class virtualization technology, and GPU-based computing in a SaaS environment.

Skills and Experience Required:
To be successful in the role of Senior ETL Engineer, you must have:
  • BS/MS degree in Computer Science or related field with 6+ years of professional application development experience
  • Minimum 4+ years of solid experience designing, building and maintaining metadata for large data warehouses using Informatica/SSIS and SQL Server/Oracle/Teradata or database appliance like Netezza, DataAllegro, etc.
  • Experience with SDK development on Informatica PowerCenter
  • Strong skills in developing data transformation for unstructured and semi-structured data 
  • Server side development experience Java/JEE
  • Sql Server Integration Services development experience
  • deployment and custom portals experience using Informatica
  • Solid understanding of OLAP and data warehousing concepts
  • Strong understanding of data models and data architecture
  • Demonstrated ability to produce quality code
  • Proven ability to work well with others in fast-paced, iterative product definition and development environment
Additionally, RMS will give additional consideration to candidates that have skills or knowledge in the following areas:
  • Working experience in financial or insurance industry
  • Experience implementing various Enterprise Integration Patterns
  • Experience implementing Informatica Web Services
  • Integration of  Informatica ETL with Java or .NET based business rules engine
  • Experience developing cross platform data orchestrations using  shell scripts, and/or a Service Oriented Architecture (SOA)
