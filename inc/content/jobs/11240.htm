Senior Software Engineer – C#
You will be part of team responsible for designing and developing the Financial Engines, framework, and modularized components under a high performance and scalable platform. As a Senior Software Engineer, this will be an opportunity to play a key role in designing, developing, and furthermore innovating together as you bring your considerable software engineering skills to our dynamically growing organization.
 
The Software Development group focuses on developing and building cutting edge, high performance enterprise software solutions that deliver risk quantification tools to our 400+ clients worldwide.  Using cutting edge technology, we are building an exciting next generation product that leverages the latest advances in database tools, grid computing, hardware-based acceleration techniques, and visualization optimization. Our Enterprise Platform utilizes restful web services, 100+ TB of data warehousing/OLAP solutions, large scale distributed data processing technology, best-in-class virtualization technology, and GPU-based computing in a SaaS environment.

  • Experience with, and excellent knowledge of the following technologies: C#, C++, SQL, WCF, .NET.
  • Excellent object oriented design skills with extensive knowledge/usage of Design patterns.
  • Strong data modeling and relational database design skills. Experience with MS SQL Server.
  • Strong interest in writing scalable, high performance applications.
  • BS/MS in Computer Science and minimum of 5 years experience.
  • Degree/courses in statistics and/or financial engineering.
  • Experience building custom application frameworks.
  • Demonstrated ability to produce quality code to standards specification.
  • Work well with others in fast-paced, iterative product definition and development environment within and across teams.
Additionally, RMS will give additional consideration to candidates that have skills or knowledge in the following areas:
  • Microsoft Development Certifications.
  • Experience with ADO.NET, COM.
  • Knowledge and use of performance monitoring, code coverage, and memory checking tools.
  • Leadership trainings in the past.
  • Experience in dealing with the interop of managed and unmanaged code.
About RMS:
Located in Newark, California Risk Management Solutions is the world’s leading provider of products and services for the quantification and management of catastrophe risk associated with earthquake, hurricane, windstorm, flood, pandemic disease and terrorism peril events. In 1988, RMS launched with an innovative approach to risk management that has since broadened to a portfolio of regional peril models and enterprise platform solutions to further solidify our position as the industry leader in catastrophic modeling and insurance risk management. Contributing to the company’s success is our ability to hire top talent and empower employees to anticipate and act on new opportunities. Even with our tremendous growth, RMS has retained its entrepreneurial culture and single-minded focus on developing industry leading models and analytic applications to help clients make the best decisions in mitigating financial risk. Today, RMS remains the industry leader in catastrophic risk modeling as we continue to build a cutting edge, high-performance Enterprise Platform to set the standard in risk management.
