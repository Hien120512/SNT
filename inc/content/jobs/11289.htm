Senior Recruiter

New Delhi, India (Noida, India) Position: 11289
RMS is the world's leading provider of analytics and decision science solutions for the quantification and management of catastrophe risks.  The models and consulting services of RMS are used by hundreds of insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations, and governments to assess a wide-range of natural and man-made perils such as - earthquake, flood, windstorm, terrorism, and disease pandemic.  RMS continues to grow and diversify in order to service and meet the requirement of its clients through research and technology innovation and superior client service.  The company's strength lies in its ability to use and develop the skills of its people across a wide remit of business activities.
 
Objective of Senior Recruiter
 
Work closely and partner with senior management, recruitment colleagues and functional HR Business Partners to drive a world-class recruitment function with the overall goal to recruit and hire exceptional talent.
 
Key Accountabilities & Deliverables:
  • Lead sourcing initiatives in a time constrained and cost efficient professional process.
  • Employs specific targeted lead generation tools for identifying functional talent at all levels of the organization.
  • Continuously maintains an accurate record of all process transactions related to a specific candidate(s).
  • Full recruitment cycle responsibility from the development of job descriptions in line with business goals and RMS competencies, to developing leading-edge methods to attract diverse talent, devising robust and consistent screening processes and successfully closing candidates.
  • Help drive the Campus hiring process and build strong University focused relationships.
  • Drive the RMS brand as an employer of choice through efficient and proactive recruitment.
  • Deliver on target staffing plans for executives on monthly, quarterly, and annual basis.
  • Cultivate and maintain relationships with passive candidates for direct recruitment.
Person Requirements:
  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent.
  • Experience working with corporate in IT sector is preferred.
  • Solid track record of sourcing or identifying relevant passive and reactive candidates for opportunities.
  • Solid track record of facilitating a robust candidate selection process and outstanding closing skills are required.
  • Self-directed and a team player with outgoing and tenacious attitude.
  • Results oriented and possess a strong sense of urgency.
  • Process orientated with efficiency focus.
  • Willingness and flexibility on working hours to accommodate international meetings outside of India’s time zones.
