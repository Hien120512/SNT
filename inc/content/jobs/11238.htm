Senior Technical Specialist (Services)

New Delhi, India (Noida, India) Position: 11238
RMS is the world's leading provider of analytics and decision science solutions for the quantification and management of catastrophe risks.  The models and consulting services of RMS are used by hundreds of insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations, and governments to assess a wide-range of natural and man-made perils such as - earthquake, flood, windstorm, terrorism, and disease pandemic.  RMS continues to grow and diversify in order to service and meet the requirement of its clients through research and technology innovation and superior client service.  The company's strength lies in its ability to use and develop the skills of its people across a wide remit of business activities.
 
Department
Platform Services Team within the Software Team
 
Objective of the Senior Technical Specialist
Will lead the platform services team in Noida and be responsible for designing and developing the frameworks for Web services / WCF services that expose the business logic components. 
 
Key Accountabilities & Deliverables:
  • Contribute to vision of, and build platform of web service APIs
  • Mentors junior engineers technically and professionally
  • Collaborate with engineers, Product/Project Managers and Quality Assurance engineers to develop multi-tier, multi-layered application layer framework and functional components, following coding, documentation and design standards
  • Innovate, design and develop extensible and reusable applications to specification, for the enterprise suite
  • Participate in integration of components / services with various products
  • Demonstrated ability to produce quality code and participate in unit/peer testing
  • Participate in internal/cross team meetings, requirements gathering, scoping, decision making and technical documentation
  • Drive toward development process improvement and implement best-practice like continuous integration
Experience/Education Required:
  • MCA / BE (Computer Science) or equivalent.  If MCA, then preference to graduation in mathematics / statistics / economics or equivalent.
  • Substantial experience professional and relevant industry experience developing multi-tier, scalable, and high performance applications using C#/Java and other .NET technologies
  • Solid understanding of SOA and 5+ year hands-on demonstrable experience in WCF, XML, SOAP, REST style Web Services using various transport protocols (HTTP, TCP)
  • Strong OO & API design experience and excellent C#/Java skills. 
  • Experience with workflow engine and/or rules engine.
  • Experience with deploying/running web service in a hosted environment.
  • Good Knowledge/Hands on of interacting with databases using ADO.NET, and expertise in SQL queries and / or LINQ
