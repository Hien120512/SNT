Consultant

New York City Metro (Hackensack, NJ, United States) Position: 11022
Company Overview
 
RMS is the world's leading provider of analytics and decision science solutions for the quantification and management of catastrophe risks.  The models and consulting services of RMS are used by hundreds of insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations, and governments to assess a wide-range of natural and man-made perils such as - hurricane, earthquake, flood, windstorm, terrorism, and disease pandemic.  RMS continues to grow and diversify in order to service and meet the need of its clients through research and technology innovation and superior client service.  The company's strength lies in its ability to use and develop the skills of its people across a wide remit of business activities.

Opportunity
 
Successfully manage complicated projects often involving other staff members:
 
  • Responsible for meeting timelines and deliverables as required
  • Strong ability to guide the analytics of projects and identify problem areas and solutions
  • Lead conversations with clients
  • Manage the resources on the project, which could include cross-departmental efforts
  • Synthesize and develop more creative, efficient approaches to meeting client needs
  • Work with senior RMS Consulting resources to scope projects and develop proposals
  • Prepare presentations and documentation
  • Effective teacher, mentor, and coach to Analysts
 
 
Essential Job Functions
 
The position requires a BA/BS or MA/MS degree with strong mathematical/quantitative/technical skills.  The ideal candidate would enjoy performing complex analyses and be comfortable presenting and explaining results to clients.  Interpersonal, communications and presentations skills are important to this role. The ability to multi-task and work on concurrent projects is essential to a candidate's success in this position. The candidate will need a high level of proficiency in data analysis and data manipulation software tools (Excel, Access, VBA and SQL). Any prior exposure or experience with RMS products and/or catastrophe modelling would be beneficial. The candidate's ability to interact, communicate and collaborate with a diverse group of people on multiple levels will enable success in this exciting position. 
 
Position Requirements 
 
The Consultant is responsible for executing risk analyses using technical, statistical, and economic tools for insurers, brokers, and other financial intermediaries for catastrophe risk management.  The Consultant carries out project management responsibilities, driving the analytics on projects by guiding Analysts.
 
  • 5+ years of analytical or quantitative work experience
  • Project management experience managing complex projects with overall responsibility for quality assurance and delivery
  • Strong analytical and business problem solving skills
  • Ability to work independently with limited supervision
  • Comfortable interacting with clients on all levels
  • Proven excellent client management skills
  • Excellent verbal and written communication
  • Strong data manipulation (MS Access, SQL) and analysis skills
  • Knowledge of statistical and mathematical concepts
  • Highly goal oriented
  • Experience of RMS products, especially RiskLink highly desirable
  • MSc preferred, but as a minimum a first degree in Finance, Economics, Engineering, Mathematics, or equivalent
