Senior Financial Modeler - Quant
 
Risk Management Solutions develops stochastic models for assessment of the financial risk due to natural catastrophic events such as windstorms, and earthquakes, as well as man-made catastrophic events such as terrorism. Research, develop, and test methods and algorithms for modeling insurance and reinsurance financial structures for RMS's software products for various natural and man-made catastrophes.

As a Senior Financial Modeler, you will focus on the following areas:
  • Research, develop, and test methods and algorithms for modeling insurance and reinsurance financial structures for RMS's software products for various natural and man-made catastrophes. 
  • Work with members of the product marketing and client development groups to determine the requirements of the financial model features. 
  • Explain the details of the financial model and consult with clients. 
  • Modeling unusual financial structures for use in software using prior experience, texts, knowledge of insurance structures and usage.
The Software Development group focuses on developing and building cutting edge, high performance enterprise software solutions that deliver risk quantification tools to our 400+ clients worldwide.  Using cutting edge technology, we are building an exciting next generation product that leverages the latest advances in database tools, grid computing, hardware-based acceleration techniques, and visualization optimization. Our Enterprise Platform utilizes restful web services, 100+ TB of data warehousing/OLAP solutions, large scale distributed data processing technology, best-in-class virtualization technology, and GPU-based computing in a SaaS environment.
 
Skills and Experience Required:
To be successful in the role of Senior Financial Modeler, you must be/have:
  • MS or Ph.D. in Math, Statistics, Engineering, or related field.
  • Strong experience in probability theory and statistics.
  • Demonstrated computer skills and a proficiency in programming languages. 
  • Ability to be creative when tackling mathematical research and development problems. 
  • Ability to think logically in order to solve internal and client problems or questions.  
  • Strong writing and spoken language skills for writing documents and giving presentations to clients. 
  • Proven ability to work in a team environment.
  • Strong programming skills and experience with high level programming languages such as C++/C#.
