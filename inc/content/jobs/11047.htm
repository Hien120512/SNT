Senior Consultant

New York City Metro (Hackensack, NJ, United States) Position: 11047

I. Position summary:
The Senior Product Marketing Manager is the day to day technical manager on UW and Data Solutions projects.  This person is responsible for developing, advising and recommending technical guidance and solutions that solve client business problems.
 
II. Essential Job Functions:
Day to day technical responsibility for meeting timelines and deliverables as required
Complete ability to provide technical analytics of projects and identify problem areas and solutions
When directed serve as the primary technical point of contact with the client
Coordinate as necessary with Product Development to understand and be proficient on new product functionalities
Synthesize and develop more creative, efficient approaches to meeting client needs
Work with RMS UW and Data Solutions resources to provide technical scope for projects and proposals
 
III. Position Requirements:
A. Job-related knowledge, skills and abilities:
3-5 years of programming, analytical and quantitative work experience
2-4 years of API and web services development experience
Project management experience involving multiple projects with overall responsibility for quality assurance and delivery
Ability to work independent with limited supervision
Data manipulation and analysis
Knowledge of statistical and mathematical concepts
Highly goal and solution orientated
 
B. Creative, problem-solving and/or communication abilities:
Strong analytical and business problem solving skills
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
 
C. Required Licenses, certifications and/or education/degrees:
Minimum first degree (BA/BS) in finance, economics, engineering, mathematics or equivalent
 
D. Other requirements (preferred);
MBA
Consulting experience
Risk management experience
Property & Casualty insurance experience
Risk transfer experience
