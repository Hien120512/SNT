Director, Natural Catastrophe Portfolio Solutions

London (London, United Kingdom) Position: 11041
RMS is the world's leading provider of analytics and decision science solutions for the quantification and management of catastrophe risks.  The models and consulting services of RMS are used by hundreds of insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations, and governments to assess a wide-range of natural and man-made perils such as - earthquake, flood, windstorm, terrorism, and disease pandemic.  RMS continues to grow and diversify in order to service and meet the requirement of its clients through research and technology innovation and superior client service.  The company's strength lies in its ability to use and develop the skills of its people across a wide remit of business activities.
 
Department
The RMS Nat Cat Portfolio Solutions team is responsible for defining RMS’ natural catastrophe model development agenda to meet market demand, ensuring commercial success of the models, and providing the outbound communication with our client base.   
 
Objective of the (Senior) Director, Nat Cat Portfolio Solutions (NCPS)
Work with NCPS leadership to develop a vision and strategy for RMS Next Generation Reinsurance solutions for underwriting and portfolio management
 
Key Accountabilities & Deliverables:
  • Gather and document market intelligence on reinsurance capital management, underwriting, pricing and portfolio management, including current practices, unmet needs, and competitor products
  • Engage with re/insurance clients and prospects on diagnostic review of current operations, understand their needs and priorities, document findings and formulating high-level solutions and requirements
  • Develop compelling proposals for development partnership engagements with clients/prospects
  • Plan and execute development partnership projects in coordination with RMS and client teams
  • Monitor progress of RMS reinsurance solutions roadmap and assist in taking these products to market
  • Coordinate with product management, software, sales and service teams to ensure successful development of products
 
Experience Required:
  • A good Quantitative Degree is essential as is the familiarity with advanced analytical, mathematical and statistical concepts.
  • Hands on operational and management experience in catastrophe risk analytics at a sophisticated reinsurer, with a demonstrated track record of advancing organizational capabilities.
  • Broad understanding of the information technology needs and implementation at a reinsurer, and active involvement in conceiving, directing or developing at least one automation application to address a key business issue.
  • Broad understanding of the reinsurance industry and a good grasp of risk management concepts.
  • Substantial experience in the insurance/ reinsurance/ broker/ consulting industry, with the most recent experience at a reinsurer.
  • Strong business acumen coupled with a consultative approach to collaborate with clients, identify needs and present compelling solutions and capabilities.
  • Quantitative mindset with awareness of statistical and mathematical concepts.
  • Inquisitive and intellectually curious. Creative in conceptualizing and building-out innovative solutions.
