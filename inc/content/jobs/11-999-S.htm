Risk Analyst Graduate Opportunity - 2011

London (London, United Kingdom) Position: 11-999-S
RMS is the world's leading provider of analytics and decision science solutions for the quantification and management of catastrophe risks.  The models and consulting services of RMS are used by hundreds of insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations, and governments to assess a wide-range of natural and man-made perils such as - hurricane, earthquake, flood, windstorm, terrorism, and disease pandemic.  RMS continues to grow and diversify in order to service and meet the need of its clients through research and technology innovation and superior client service.  The company's strength lies in its ability to use and develop the skills of its people across a wide remit of business activities.
 
The Opportunity
The Risk Analyst Graduate Training Scheme is designed to prepare you for a career in risk management, by providing a solid grounding in approaches to catastrophe risk quantification and management for the insurance and capital markets.  
 
Whilst the scheme lasts for twelve months this is a permanent opportunity and is a combination of:
  • Structured training given over a four to six week period before moving to your team
  • Hands-on experience via secondments through various RMS practice teams including the global product service team where you will receive training on our key products and client services
  • Potentially a client placement
 
The interactive structured training is delivered by a number of RMS colleagues, including Executive team members, group leads, and also previous graduates of the Training Scheme, all of whom have particular areas of expertise and knowledge. The structured training typically takes the form of presentations involving follow-up projects and exercises.
 
During the client placement you will have the opportunity to work on-site with an RMS client, joining their teams of dedicated catastrophe modelling and management staff. This will allow you to experience first-hand how our clients employ RMS technologies within their underwriting, portfolio management and capital allocation processes.
 
 
The Teams:

RiskMarkets
RMS RiskMarkets specialises in helping clients manage the transfer of catastrophe risk to the capital markets.  Through a combination of consulting services, portfolio management products, and indices for trading, RiskMarkets works closely with all sectors of the catastrophe risk market.    
 
The RMS RiskMarkets team has expanded rapidly over the last few years, responding to the fast increase in demand for insurance linked securities and the appetite for the purchase of this form of risk in the capital markets.  We look at this area as a significant opportunity to be a core player in the growth of a unique and fascinating market, working across our diverse range of offerings.
 
 
Client Development
RMS Client Development is responsible for managing our key client relationships across the insurance, reinsurance and brokerage marketplace.  It houses our Sales team, Global Sales Support team (GSS), Client Services team, Product Support and Deployment teams.  Like the rest of RMS Client development is still growing with significant opportunities for graduates in the client services and GSS departments.
 
Person Specification 
  • Strong mathematical ability
  • Good BSc or MSc in a quantitative or scientific subject
  • Fast learner, entrepreneurial and strong communicator
  • Interest in the capital markets & finance would be advantageous for RiskMarkets
