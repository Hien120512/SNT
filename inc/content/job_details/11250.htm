Account Manager

Zurich (Zurich, Switzerland) Position: 11250
RMS is the world's leading provider of analytics and decision science solutions for the quantification and management of catastrophe risks.  The models and consulting services of RMS are used by hundreds of insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations, and governments to assess a wide-range of natural and man-made perils such as - earthquake, flood, windstorm, terrorism, and disease pandemic.  RMS continues to grow and diversify in order to service and meet the requirement of its clients through research and technology innovation and superior client service.  The company's strength lies in its ability to use and develop the skills of its people across a wide remit of business activities.
 
Department
Due to substantial product and service growth RMS seeks to hire an Account Manager for its Global Service organization. The role sits within the Regional Service team at RMS in Zurich, which is focused on providing high quality account management and technical product support to RMS’ insurance, reinsurance and broker clients in Continental Europe (CE).
 
Objective of the Account Manager
Service and account management responsibility for 3 – 10 clients in their application and use of RMS models and products ensuring high quality solutions to client business challenges.
 
Key Accountabilities & Deliverables:
  • Service and account management
o   Maintain regular meetings with clients to review the business needs and any open service issues
o   Ensure RMS provides timely response to bugs, fixes and queries
o   Achieve high level of client satisfaction (quantitative and qualitative)
  • Advise CE clients on the application and usage of RMS’ natural and man-made catastrophe models, applications and services
o   Help clients understand the scientific and engineering assumptions built into the peril models
o   Provide client training on RMS products
o   Work with clients on estimating losses from large catastrophe events
o   Identification and development of solutions, using existing, new and bespoke technologies
  • Overall responsibility for success of client change management initiatives during new model releases
o   Explain concepts and practices of new models and their impact on data, output etc.
o   Analyze and manipulate client exposure data and loss results and present outcome to client
  • Co-coordinate interaction between clients and RMS business units for product development
  • Participate in internal product development discussions and design
  • Introduce new products and services to clients and identify and report potential sales opportunities to Sales organization
  • Work with an international client base
  • Whilst based in Zurich, travel to other RMS and client offices is required from time to time
