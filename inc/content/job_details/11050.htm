Business Development Lead for Life & Health Risk Analytics

London (London, United Kingdom) Position: 11050
RMS is the world's leading provider of analytics and decision science solutions for the quantification and management of catastrophe risks. The models and consulting services of RMS are used by hundreds of insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations, and governments to assess a wide-range of natural and man-made perils. RMS is expanding beyond its historical core of catastrophe modeling in the property and casualty sector into life insurance, pension risk management and capital markets, analyzing mortality patterns, threats such as pandemics, and the causes of longevity risk. RMS continues to grow and diversify in order to service and meet the requirement of its clients through research and technology innovation and superior client service. The company's strength lies in its ability to use and develop the skills of its people across a wide remit of business activities.
 
Department
The Emerging Risk Solutions (ERS) team at RMS develops risk management products and is currently addressing opportunities in modeling mortality risk for financial services sectors relating to life and health insurance and pension risks. The ERS team is responsible for:
  • Models that quantify excess mortality risk including:
o   RMS Infectious Disease Model (influenza pandemic and emerging infectious diseases)
o   RMS Terrorism Risk Model
o   Other mass casualty perils such as natural catastrophes: earthquakes and tsunamis
  • RMS Longevity Risk Model
  • RMS medical-based mortality risk modeling
  • Other models of hedging, financial structures and base mortality risk
Our life & health risk modeling team consists of medical-based specialists, mathematicians, epidemiologists, statisticians and other areas of bio-science expertise, working with product development teams including software architects, coding engineers and designers, and with market development personnel who liaise with clients to ensure the commercial value of models being developed. The team is based in California, and London, with software development in India.
 
Objective of the Business Development Lead
The primary goal of the Business Development Lead is to build the RMS brand in providing risk analytics to the Life Insurance, pensions and annuities market in the UK and Europe.  This will be accomplished through expanding current RMS client relationships, developing new client relationships, identifying and exploiting new distribution channels for RMS technology and forming strategic partnerships to accelerate the development of RMS technology in this new space.  The Business Development Lead will also seek strategic opportunities to raise awareness of RMS capabilities in the market place through a variety of marketing activities.  Booking responsibilities for Europe/UK contributing to the ERS team’s targeted growth of 30%-40% per year in the near term.  Sales will consist of a combination of consulting work and the licensing of RMS models for longevity risk, mortality, flu pandemic and other products as they develop over time.  In addition, there is expected to be work in the Capital Markets related to the securitization Life & Health risk.
