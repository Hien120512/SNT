Objective of the Senior Software Engineer – (Services)
Will be part of team responsible for designing and developing the business logic components and services for the next generation software suite being developed for Windows using Microsoft technology.  As a Senior Software Engineer, this will be an opportunity to play a key role in designing, developing, and furthermore innovating together.   
 
Key Accountabilities & Deliverables:
  • Provide a senior-level contribution to a team responsible for the design, development and implementation of business logic components and services
  • Collaborate with engineers, Product/Project Managers and Quality Assurance engineers to develop multi-tier, multi-layered application layer framework and functional components, following coding, documentation and design standards
  • Innovate, design and develop extensible and reusable applications to specification, for the enterprise suite
  • Participate in integration of components / services with various products
  • Demonstrated ability to produce quality code and participate in unit/peer testing
  • Participate in internal/cross team meetings, requirements gathering, scoping, decision making and technical documentation
  • Contribute to and/or assist in other activities such as UI design/development, database design, deployment/installation of components and services
