Objective of the QA Engineer (Testing)
 
This position will be required to work in a fast paced environment with a multi-disciplinary team of analytical professionals working on the commercial and residential building attributes data product. The candidate must be able to communicate effectively within and outside organization.  The quality assurance engineer will be responsible for testing the item under various working conditions or for verifying the information that the item provides in the case of computer software programs. Creativity, accurate record keeping, attention to detail and the overall understanding of the product are considered highly desirable for the position.
Key Accountabilities & Deliverables:
 
  • MS SQL, PL/SQL, TSQL queries testing and optimization
  • Testing of internal analytical tools, customized third party software/tools and ETL tools
  • Participation in Requirement Analysis and understating as per internal/external Project Requirement.
  • Ownership of different Testing modules
  • Specifies test methods for Application software and large database verification and validation
  • Tracking and reporting defects using appropriate tools
  • Effective involvement in Impact analysis
  • Integration analysis
  • Technical documentation (Internal program documentation, program specifications, user manual etc.)
