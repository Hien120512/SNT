Account Associate, Global Product Support

London (London, United Kingdom) Position: 11186
RMS is the world's leading provider of analytics and decision science solutions for the quantification and management of catastrophic risks throughout the world.  The models and consulting services of RMS are used by hundreds of insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations, and governments to assess a wide-range of natural and man-made perils such as - earthquake, flood, hurricane, windstorm, wild fire, disease pandemic.  RMS continues to grow and diversify in order to service and meet the requirement of its clients through research and technology innovation and superior client service.  The company’s strength lies in its ability to use and develop the skills of its people across a wide remit of business activities.
 
Department
Reporting to the Product Support Manager, the Account Associate is an integral part of the overall client satisfaction experience.  The role sits within the Global Product Support group where it is often the first point of contact for many of our clients who have enquiries about any aspect of RMS’ products and services.  The team is very much the starting point of where we add value to the client relationship.
 
Key Responsibilities
  • You will be directly responsible for all communications to clients based around the product support aspect of our service. 
  • You will gather information from our Global client base and RMS departments in order to perform tests using RMS products to solve client issues and questions.
  • Maintain full understanding of RMS models, including the ability to run the software and resolve issues relating to all aspects of our model suite.
  • Monitor the progress of client enquiries to ensure and confirm final resolution internally and/or externally. 
  • Responsible for disseminating new knowledge or feedback of product related issues to other RMS staff or clients as necessary. 
  • Provide assistance to the product development effort and execute on special projects as directed. 
  • Ability to travel to our client base where required.
 
Candidate Qualifications
  • Must have a good quantitive Degree ideally in earth sciences, maths, financial engineering or computer science
  • Working knowledge of SQL and programming is essential
  • Previous product-support related experience would be a distinct advantage
  • Knowledge of risk or catastrophe modeling is highly desired but not essential as in-depth training will be given.
  • Strong problem-solving aptitude and excellent communication skills required, ideally gained within a client facing environment.
  • Ability to work autonomously and collaboratively
  • Excellent time management and organisational skills are essential in order to meet the challenging demands of our global client base.
  • Willingness to learn new technologies
