Security Architect
The RMS Security Architect will oversee the overall product security across multiple tiers: web/client, web service, backend engine, database, etc.
 
The Software Development group focuses on developing and building cutting edge, high performance enterprise software solutions that deliver risk quantification tools to our 400+ clients worldwide.  Using cutting edge technology, we are building an exciting next generation product that leverages the latest advances in database tools, grid computing, hardware-based acceleration techniques, and visualization optimization. Our Enterprise Platform utilizes restful web services, 100+ TB of data warehousing/OLAP solutions, large scale distributed data processing technology, best-in-class virtualization technology, and GPU-based computing in a SaaS environment.
 
Skills and Experience Required:
To be successful in the role of Security Architect, you must be/have:
  • 5+ years experience with enterprise security integration such as Active Directory and LDAP
  • 5+ years experience with application authentication and authorization design and implementation
  • 5+ years experience with driving security initiatives across multiple development teams
  • 5+ years experience driving security best-practices and initiatives for a hosted SaaS environment
About RMS:
Located in Newark, California, RMS is the world’s leading provider of products and services for the quantification and management of catastrophe risk associated with earthquake, hurricane, windstorm, flood, pandemic disease and terrorism peril events. In 1988, RMS launched with an innovative approach to risk management that has since broadened to a portfolio of regional peril models and enterprise platform solutions to further solidify our position as the industry leader in catastrophic modeling and insurance risk management. Contributing to the company’s success is our ability to hire top talent and empower employees to anticipate and act on new opportunities. Even with our tremendous growth, RMS has retained its entrepreneurial culture and single-minded focus on developing industry leading models and analytic applications to help clients make the best decisions in mitigating financial risk. Today, RMS remains the industry leader in catastrophic risk modeling as we continue to build a cutting edge, high-performance Enterprise Platform to set the standard in risk management.
