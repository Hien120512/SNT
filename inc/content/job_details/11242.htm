San Francisco Area (Newark, CA, United States) Position: 11242
Lead Quality Assurance Engineer
 
RMS is a team of people with backgrounds in mathematics, civil engineering and computer science.  We create products for assessing risks from natural and manmade catastrophes for a global customer base.
 
RMS is looking for a Lead Quality Assurance Engineer to validate functioning of products against relational databases and data warehouse.
 
Responsibilities:
  • Create a new test framework for RMS’ Next Generation products with emphasis on databases and data warehousing.
  • Conceptualize, and implement automated tests of insurance business functionalities for RMS’ Next Generation Applications for Catastrophe Risk Management.
  • Work closely with development and product management to understand the database architecture and identify, design or enhance test frameworks to support backend test development.
  • Mentor more junior team members.
Required Skills
  • Proven experience in white box testing, unit testing frameworks (such as NUnit, Pex, MS Test or related), API test automation or automation framework development.  Focus on test automation with Restful web services.
  • Strong programming skills in languages such as VB.net, C++, and/or C# with good knowledge of Object Oriented concepts
  • Understanding of DBMS fundamentals and strong working knowledge of SQL is a must, along with hands-on database experience.  A minimum of 5 years working with DBMS technology as either a QA Engineer for a database server/kernel development organization, or a database application developer.
  • Experience with Business Intelligence, data warehousing and cloud computing plus.
  • Expert knowledge of database management, data structures, relational database and transactional database design, and stored procedures.
  • Experience with scripting languages such as Perl or Python. Experience developing on .Net Framework.  Web development experience a plus.
  • Excellent organizational and communication skills.  Works well independently and in a team environment.  Flexible, adapts to change.  Works well in a fast-paced culture.
  • Comfortable with the Agile methodology.
  • BS/MS in computer science and 5 years professional work experience.
 
Founded at Stanford University in 1988, Risk Management Solutions (RMS) is the world's leading provider of modeling, analytics and decision science solutions for the quantification and management of catastrophic risks throughout the world.  RMS products and services are used by hundreds of insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations and governments to assess a wide range of natural and man-made perils, from earthquakes and hurricanes to terrorism and disease pandemic. Our solutions are trusted reliable benchmarks for strategic pricing, risk management, and risk transfer decisions.
