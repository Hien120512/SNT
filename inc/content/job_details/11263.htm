Risk Analyst, Noida
 
Company
 
RMS is the world's leading provider of analytics and decision science solutions for the quantification and management of catastrophe risks.  The models and consulting services of RMS are used by hundreds of insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations, and governments to assess a wide-range of natural and man-made perils such as - earthquake, flood, windstorm, terrorism, and disease pandemic.  RMS continues to grow and diversify in order to service and meet the requirement of its clients through research and technology innovation and superior client service.  The company's strength lies in its ability to use and develop the skills of its people across a wide remit of business activities.
 
Position
 
Risk Analyst in RMSI Analytical Services
 
Department
 
RMSI Analytical Services is a highly skilled and client centric group of analysts and managers who work with insurance companies and brokers in the US, Europe and around the world to help them manage catastrophic risk. Focussing primarily on earthquake, hurricane, flood and terrorism risk, RMSI Analytical Services works closely with our clients to develop and implement solutions and reporting to provide high quality and high value deliverables which meet their precise needs.
 
The group is structured around specific teams who focus on a single client while providing support for others when the needs arise, which requires our analysts to be highly flexible and open to new challenges and new learning opportunities. Our comprehensive training scheme provides all of the expertise required to become an integral part of the team and to contribute to the highest level in both analytical and client facing roles. Our relationship with RMS, our parent company, in California provides deep insight into the market that we are serving and also provides travel opportunities for appropriate employees for short term assignments at one of RMS’s 10 offices around the world or onsite with clients.
 
We are looking for highly driven and independent analysts who are keen to learn about the catastrophe insurance market and bring their own skills, experience and enthusiasm to move the group forward in its mandate to become a truly indispensible service for our clients.
 
Objective of the Risk Analyst
 
As a Risk Analyst, the applicant will be highly involved in the manipulation of client’s data and quantification of associated risk using a variety of database tools, analyzing and interpreting data using RMS technologies and trademark software products as well as working with other RMS departments and directly with clients.
 
Key Accountabilities & Deliverables
 
  • Demonstrate good understanding of catastrophe modeling after structured training.
  • Learn best practices in catastrophe modeling and reflect this in your day to day work.
  • Participate actively in your project activities and maintain highest standards of quality of work.
  • Demonstrate understanding of RMS Products including new products as they are released.
  • Demonstrate understanding about the focus markets for RMS and the reasons why clients use the different RMS products and services.
•        Contribute towards and compile content for internal or external client reports.
  • Perform meticulous project documentation of all aspects of a project, particularly focusing on the rationale for decisions and exceptions.
  • Provide input for process improvements in at least one client project which addresses a prior or foreseeable concern or increases efficiency.
  • Support the client in understanding the output of analysis, comparing different model results and explain to the client.
