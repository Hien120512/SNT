San Francisco Area (Newark, CA, United States) Position: 11215
Distributed System Architect
As the Distributed System Architect your responsibilities will be to:
  • Oversee RMS platform team’s distributed data generation and processing technology stack
  • Work with a team responsible for the design, development, and implementation of a massively scalable system that handles TB of data with thousands of CPU cores and multi-GB/second I/O throughput
  • Collaborating with engineers, Product/Project Managers and Quality Assurance engineers to develop the system with an agile methodology
  • Participate in internal/cross team meetings, requirements gathering, scoping, decision making and technical documentation
The Software Development group focuses on developing and building cutting edge, high performance enterprise software solutions that deliver risk quantification tools to our 400+ clients worldwide.  Using cutting edge technology, we are building an exciting next generation product that leverages the latest advances in database tools, grid computing, hardware-based acceleration techniques, and visualization optimization. Our Enterprise Platform utilizes restful web services, 100+ TB of data warehousing/OLAP solutions, large scale distributed data processing technology, best-in-class virtualization technology, and GPU-based computing in a SaaS environment.
 
Skills and Experience Required: 
The successful Distributed System Architect will have:
  • BS/MS degree in Computer Science or related field with 10+ years of relevant industry experience
  • Solid experience designed and implementing distributed data system (eg. Cassandra, Hadoop, Microsoft HPC, parallel database)
  • Solid experience working on scalable architectures including distributed file system & distributed cache
  • Strong experience with performance and scalability in a distributed environment
  • Experience with cloud computing and Saas
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
 
About RMS:

In 1988, RMS launched with an innovative approach to risk management that has since broadened to a portfolio of regional peril models and enterprise platform solutions to further solidify our position as the industry leader in catastrophic modeling and insurance risk management. Even with our tremendous growth, RMS has retained its entrepreneurial culture and single-minded focus on developing industry leading models and analytic applications to help clients make the best decisions in mitigating financial risk. Today, RMS remains the industry leader in catastrophic risk modeling as we continue to build a cutting edge, high-performance Enterprise Platform to set the standard in risk management.
