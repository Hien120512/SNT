New York City Metro (Hackensack, NJ, United States) Position: 11100
Technical Consultant - Architect
Job Description Overview
A Technical Consultant’s primary focus is on improving and innovating the way RMS delivers our products and services.  The individual’s primary responsibility will be the deployment and support of RMS products.  The individual will lead and participate in simple to complex deployments of RMS products.  This includes, but is not limited to product release packaging, configuration design, testing, distribution, and support.  This individual will lead and support the development and evolution of processes, guidelines, best practices and toolsets.    
This individual will also support Product Marketing, Sales, Service, and Product Management initiatives.  For example, the individual will support Product Marketing by providing feedback from clients on requests for new product capabilities, and by developing standalone/integrated product demonstrations that showcase the capabilities of our products.  The individual will support Sales by supporting proposal initiatives, conducting product overview presentations, conduct client interviews, by performing scoping and planning activities, and by developing proposal content.  The individual will support Client Services by providing system/environment management support and by developing support knowledge content.  The Individual will support Product Management by providing feedback on deficiencies, limitations, and technical environment requirements.   
Special knowledge, skills and ability requirements:
The individual must be a self-directed individual with the ability to perform in cross-functional, multi-organizational and virtual teams.  The individual will be client facing and will need to present themselves in a professional manor.  The individual may be required to travel and manage their related time and expenses.  The individual must be experienced in conducting interviews, identifying sources of information, analyzing data, performing evaluations, developing recommendations and possess strong oral, written and presentation skills.  The individual must possess a competence in systems analysis, systems design, project management, client management, systems deployment and in providing customer service support.  The individual must be process oriented and experienced in project, quality, issue and risk management methodologies. 
The individual must posses a strong competence, and should have experience with the following foundation technologies that RMS products are built on: Windows Server and Desktop Operating Systems, SQL Server, and T-SQL. 
Specifically, the individual must possess the following technical skills:
  • Strong competence on Windows Server system administration.
  • Proficiency in SQL Server including, but not limited to, database administration, configuration, performance tuning, data structures, and T-SQL.
  • Proven competence in issue resolution and management.
  • Develop IT Infrastructure recommendation to support the deployment of RMS products.
  • Awareness of networking protocols, their limits and requirements, including client/server and n-tier architectures.
  • Awareness and application of disaster recovery and security principles for networking and data storage.
  • Develop and execute test plans.
  • Develop reports using data mining, analysis, and reporting tools.
  • Proficiency in MS Office products is required
  • Preference will be given to individuals with Microsoft Server 2008 HPC and/or experience with virtualization technologies.
 
Education and experience required:
It is highly desirable that the individual posses an information technology, logical science or business four-year degree.  The individual must have 3 – 5 years experience in deploying and supporting business applications. The individual must have 3 – 5 years experience working within a structured project management and application development lifecycle framework.
