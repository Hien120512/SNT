Sr. Marketing Analyst

New York City Metro (Hackensack, NJ, United States) Position: 11023
The Marketing Analyst, Underwriting and Data Solutions, is responsible for assisting in the creation and execution of the UWDS  marketing strategy to increase RMS’s presence and value proposition in the P&C insurance underwriting space. This includes marketing research and programs aimed at improving awareness and image in the various segments (e.g. homeowners, small business, middle market, large commercial, industrial); marketing established as well as newly developed solutions; executing marketing strategies for new markets and increasing market share in existing markets; enabling our sales teams to appropriately position our suite of Next Generation underwriting solutions; and ultimately helping RMS achieve its ambitious sales and growth targets.
 
Specific responsibilities
  • Define, conduct and report on market research and competitive analyses related to the development and validation of the UWDS product roadmap and marketing strategies for each segment (e.g. homeowners, small commercial, middle market, large commercial, industrial) consistent with the RMS strategy and inclusive of company image and core capabilities (data solutions, catastrophe modeling and analytics, embeddable services, and sophisticated applications)
  • Assist in the development and execution of go-to-market plans for UWDS products and services, including appropriate change management programs in support of existing RMS clients.
  • Build a strong rapport with Client Development, Software Product Management, and other key teams within RMS in support of the development and execution of UWDS marketing strategies.
  •  Assist in the creation of relevant sales assets, including but not limited to brochures, press releases, technical briefs and white papers, customer success stories, web/webinar content, presentations, and events.
  • Analyze the existing RMS client base to identify usage and other patterns and opportunities.
  • Collaborate with RMS’ Corporate Marketing team in support of RMS’ underwriting solutions agenda, leading to improved generation of sales leads and sales
  • Help to develop business cases for additional product investments, and to prioritize multiple opportunities against relevant economic and strategic criteria

  • 2 years software marketing experience
  • Knowledge of commercial insurance underwriting and/or the P&C insurance industry is a plus
  • Competent in MS Office products (Word, Excel, Powerpoint)
Personal
  • Exceptional oral and written communication skills, to enable effective communication with all levels of the organization and with RMS clients
  • Expertise in translating marketing strategies into tangible, relevant sales assets
  • A strong team player who can function  across multiple constituencies and actively contribute to cross-functional teams
  • Collaborative and client-focused, enthusiastic and willing to speak up and contribute
  • Well-organized, productive, able to multi-task effectively and with an eye for detail
