RMS
RMS is the world’s leading provider of products and services for the quantification and management of catastrophe risks associated with perils such as earthquakes, hurricanes, windstorms, flood and terrorism.  Founded in 1988, the Company was instrumental in creating the “cat modeling” approach to risk management, a new analytical software category that did not previously exist.  Over the years, RMS has broadened its portfolio of regional peril models and solidified its position as the leader in the field.    In addition to being the largest licensor of catastrophe peril models, the Company has also become a leading provider of hazard and exposure data and data quality improvement services.  RMS is also a software application vendor, building applications that enable clients not only to run RMS models but also to better understand exposures and loss projection output.  More than 400 insurers, reinsurers, brokers and other financial institutions around the world utilize RMS models to quantify, manage and transfer risk. 
 
Contributing to the Company’s success is its ability to hire top talent and empower employees to anticipate and act on new opportunities in a nimble and agile manner.  Despite its tremendous growth, RMS has retained its entrepreneurial culture and its single-minded focus on developing leading-edge models and analytic applications to help its insurance industry clients make better risk management decisions. 
 
The company is headquartered in Newark, California in Silicon Valley and employs over 1,500 people situated in all the world’s financial centers, including the U.S., U.K., Bermuda, France, Switzerland, India and Japan.  RMS is subsidiary of the U.K.-based Daily Mail and General Trust (DMGT), plc.  DMGT is listed on the London Stock Exchange.
 
RMS Consulting
 
RMS Consulting has a proven track record in providing catastrophe-related advisory services to senior management in the re/insurance sectors and associated industries.  We focus both on improving companies’ workflows and strengthening their positions in their respective marketplace.  As such, we carry out a wide array of projects for our clients, ranging from small bespoke analytical projects to large process and strategic consulting engagements. 
Our services also extend internally as we work with RMS product development teams and advise them with developing next generation products and services. The RMS Consulting team works directly under the Underwriting and Data Solutions (UWDS) business unit, which has two primary roles:
  • Business management – identify opportunities; determine solution strategies, critical product features, and priorities
  • Product marketing – marketing, sales campaigns, and sales support
 
Job description
Analysts help develop and implement customized analyses and solutions incorporating clients’ business needs and resources. This includes:
  • Assisting with the planning, design, and implementation of RMS’s underwriting and data solutions for assigned consulting projects
  • Providing input to UWDS to help identify additional client requirements that should be addressed by RMS in terms of data, models, analytics, applications, and technology (IT infrastructure, data warehousing, and other client IT integration points); helping to determine the best way to address these needs as part of the UWDS development process
Specific responsibilities
  • Understand and use RMS models and tools to analyze and characterize client portfolios 
  • Provide analyses and presentation support for consulting projects
  • Work with cross-functional and cross-regional teams within RMS to develop and test future products and services
  • Help with sales initiatives by demonstrating products, scoping out projects and developing proposals
