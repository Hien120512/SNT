Senior Systems Analyst

London (London, United Kingdom) Position: 11241
RMS is the world's leading provider of analytics and decision science solutions for the quantification and management of catastrophe risks.  The models and consulting services of RMS are used by hundreds of insurance and reinsurance companies, hedge funds, corporations, and governments to assess a wide-range of natural and man-made perils such as - earthquake, flood, windstorm, terrorism, and disease pandemic.  RMS continues to grow and diversify in order to service and meet the requirement of its clients through research and technology innovation and superior client service.  The company's strength lies in its ability to use and develop the skills of its people across a wide remit of business activities.
 
Department
Part of the Enterprise Services support team the Information Technology department supports the RMS business in London.
 
Objective of the Senior Systems Analyst 
The Application and Model developers who create our scientific models rely on a HPC cluster to assist in their compute, research and analysis needs. Reporting to the Director of Regional Operations this position has the primary responsibility of creating vision, execution and maintenance of the HPC compute cluster, how it is used and accessed across our Model development teams.
 
A key success to your role will be working across organizational boundaries and working closely with our Model Development organization. Through this close collaboration you will gain a better understanding of the complexities and subtleties they face which will help you design and implement technology solutions to improve their development timescales.  Thus, you will be needed for the long haul as Model creation is a multi-year effort.
 
This is an exciting and challenging postion for an individual who is simply not just looking for another IT role, but a role which has autononmy, growth and knowledge that you are significantly contributing  to the creation of the next generation of Catastrophe Models used by many of the world leading insurer’s, reinsurers and brokers.
 
RMS has, and continues, to invest significantly in resources which improve the quality, granularity and depth of our products and services and we’d be very interested to talk to you if you’re up to the challenge
 
 
 
Key Accountabilities & Deliverables:
 
  • Perform system administration of company-wide Linux and HPC environment – maintenance, performance and issue resolution.
  • Development and alignment of System and Application Development Roadmaps.
  • Implementation of performance metrics to measure system and application demands / bottlenecks.
  • Perform and monitor server backup and off-site data replication.
  • Researching and recommending equipment and software solutions for to enable effective use of budgets.

  • Experience in the financial services or academia fields would be beneficial.
  • Experience configuring and maintaining secure, large-scale, complex, distributed applications/platforms.
  • Experience developing/implementing centralized management, monitoring and deployment tools like xCAT and Nagios.
  • Experience of SAN, Fibre Channel, Infiniband, NFS, PostgreSQL.
  • Deep experience of managing GPFS filesystems, Sun Grid Engine, TSM and HSM.
  • Ideally, you will have previous programming experience in Shell, C, Fortran, Perl or Python.
